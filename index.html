Get addicted to e-road bikes | The Sky is the limit

When we decided to develop the Addict RC with electric drive, we didn't want to make road cycling "easier" per se. We simply wanted to make it better. There's no question about it: light, stiff and at least as stylish as its World Tour equivalent, the Addict eRIDE outshines everything else in global cycling.



CABLE INTEGRATION

All 4 cables are internally routed, regardless of whether electronic or mechanical drives are preferred. No cables are visible from the outside. Likewise, no screws are visible due to the integrated magnetic stem cap.

HIGHER-TACKLE

To be able to tackle higher, longer and even steeper climbs - that was our goal. So that you can set off even earlier and only hit home again later.

CONSTRUCTION

Carbon construction is at the heart of Syncros. The unique shape features smooth transitions without sharp edges, especially at the transition between stem and handlebar. Carbon is best suited for flowing lines in terms of both strength and stiffness

STIFFNESS

This specific and unique V-shape also allows a better integration of the computer closer in front of the rider.

COMPUTER INTEGRATION

This specific and unique V-shape also allows better integration of the computer closer in front of the driver.

ERGONOMICS

In collaboration with our ergonomics partner gebioMized, we have optimized accordingly the tops with oval shape to avoid pressure points, the hoods with smooth transition, the palm support and drops with compact shape. Even the graphics are designed for extra grip if you decide to ride without handlebar tape on the tops
